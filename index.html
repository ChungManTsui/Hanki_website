About Us

Authentic Korean food brought to you by our professional chef who has over 40 years of experience in Korean cuisine. Variety of options from light meal to heavy sauced meal, tonnes of fresh vegetable options to meaty fried chickens and from cold food to warm soup to choose from.

Every ingredient is selected carefully by our chef to deliver only the best to your table. We pride ourselves on authenticity, quality, and exceptional service.
